An app for helping NEAR Whales (narwhals) carry their heavy bags of tokens. Poor narwhals!

Are you a narwhal, too?

Do you have several buckets full of NEAR tokens?

Do you have these spread out across multiple hardware and/or software wallets?

Do you already have trouble keeping track of all that, and then a need to keep track of lockup contracts, delegation decisions, staking rewards, and starting balances?

This app is for you!

Click the "Edit accounts" button below to get started. You can add your account data and see a dashboard of useful information about the status of your various accounts.

THIS IS ABSOLUTELY PRIVATE.

The information you enter here is never sent to any servers. It is not recorded in any way, except in your own browser, using a technology that's hard to hack. The biggest risk to your data will be your computer eventually crashing and losing it, or you clearing your browser storage. You can protect against this by copying & pasting the data you enter into multiple browsers & devices, and sending your data to a loved one or two using a secure messaging app like Signal so they can do the same. it.
